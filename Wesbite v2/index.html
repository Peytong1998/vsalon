Cancellation Policies

Please let me know 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Cancellations within 24 hours will result in a 50% fee, payable at the time of cancellation.

No-Call, No-Show

I understand that life can get busy, but please remember that a no call, no show means missed opportunities for both you and I. In the event of a no call, no show, you'll be charged 100% of the service total. This fee is due on the day of the missed appointment to ensure fairness to myself and other clients.

Your cooperation helps me keep the salon running smoothly for everyone!

Thank you for understanding. I look forward to seeing you in my chair!
